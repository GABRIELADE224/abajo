About Me

Dedicated to Environmental Sustainability & Public Health

Hailing from Imodi Ijebu, Odogbolu Local Government Area of Ogun State, I am a certified and practicing Environmental Health Officer with a relentless commitment to learning and impact.

My educational journey reflects excellence at every level—from graduating as the best student in my set at Ogun State College of Health Technology to earning a Master's degree in Environmental Management and Protection from FUNAAB.

Currently serving at the Ogun State Ministry of Environment, I specialize in policy development, environmental impact assessments, stakeholder consultations, and coordinating trainings that shape environmental governance across the state.
